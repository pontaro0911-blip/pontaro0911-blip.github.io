Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the personal, hobby-scale software application operated by an individual seller (the “Operator”) handles data when it connects to the eBay APIs. The application is used solely by the Operator to research prices and to prepare the Operator’s own eBay listing drafts. It is not a public service and has no other users.

1. Data we do NOT collect or store

The application does not collect, store, process, sell, or share any personal data of eBay users, buyers, or any other third parties. It does not maintain a database of eBay user information. It does not receive, request, or retain buyers’ names, addresses, contact details, or order/payment information beyond what eBay itself displays transiently to the Operator inside eBay’s own interface.

2. Data the application does handle

The application stores, only on the Operator’s own local computer, the following items needed to function:

  • The Operator’s own eBay OAuth tokens (access/refresh tokens) granting the Operator access to the Operator’s own eBay seller account.
  • The Operator’s own product research notes and listing drafts (titles, item specifics, prices) for items the Operator intends to sell.

These items relate to the Operator’s own account only. They are kept locally, are not transmitted to any third party, and are not published anywhere.

3. eBay Marketplace Account Deletion / Closure Notifications

Because the application does not store any eBay user’s personal data, it has no eBay user data to delete. Accordingly, the application qualifies for the exemption from eBay’s Marketplace Account Deletion/Closure Notification workflow and does not operate an account-deletion notification endpoint. Should the application ever begin storing eBay users’ personal data in the future, this policy will be updated and a compliant deletion-notification endpoint will be implemented before doing so.

4. Third parties

The application communicates only with eBay’s official APIs (for the Operator’s own account) and is not integrated with advertising networks, analytics services, or any data brokers. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Data retention & security

Locally stored tokens and notes remain on the Operator’s computer until the Operator deletes them. Access to the computer is controlled by the Operator. The Operator may revoke the application’s access at any time from the eBay account settings, after which the stored tokens become invalid.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to: pontaro0911@gmail.com
